about us

Fantastic.ng hosts match day contests across the five major European leagues (English, French, Spanish, Italian, German) and the UEFA Champions league.


Each contest will specify the parameters so for example one EPL contest might be just for the games on a saturday while another might include games on saturday, sunday and monday. In the first example, the winner is known at the end of the last game on saturday. In the second example, the contest would end and winner declared after the last game on monday night.
